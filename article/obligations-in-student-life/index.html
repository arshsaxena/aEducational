The primary obligation of student life to learn and acquire knowledge. As a student, we must do all our work at the right moment and maintain punctuality and discipline. We should remember that if we will be successful in our career and our character has a strong base, we would be able to shine in any sphere of life and would serve our society and country.

As a student, we spend most of the time in reading and learning but we should not be a book worm, always engaged in studies. We should be careful about our health and should give some time for sports, games, and other physical activities daily.

As a student, we must learn good qualities like obedience, dutifulness, respect our parents, teacher, and elders, love and sympathy towards fellow people in the society. We are the future generation of our country, therefore, we should always be good citizens, so that we can serve our country for a long time.
